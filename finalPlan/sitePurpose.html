Temple Inn & Suites Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote and provide information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers to meet the needs of temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or who participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.


Domain: templeinnandsuites.com

Target Audience

The target audience for this website are temple workers, patrons, and their families. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.
